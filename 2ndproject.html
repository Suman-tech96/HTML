NATURE

Nature encompasses all living and non-living things that exist naturally on Earth, including plants, animals, landscapes, and the elements. It's a dynamic system where living organisms interact with their physical surroundings, maintaining an ecological balance. Nature provides essential resources for life, such as oxygen, food, and water, and its beauty and diversity inspire awe and wonder. However, human activities are impacting nature, leading to environmental degradation and climate change.

  • Living organisms:Animals, plants, microorganisms, and humans are all part of the intricate web of life within nature.
  • Non-living elements:These include the atmosphere, climate, water, rocks, landforms, and geological formations.
